## Scientific-discipline checklist (enforced in the text)

- G0B is described as a **calibrated qualitative demonstration**, never an exact
  reproduction, with no N-dependence claim.
- No claim that switching beats the average graph; the diagnostic G1-strict
  values (negative in 8/8 seeds) are labelled NOT_INTERPRETABLE and never promoted.
- G1-weak is reported as **directional evidence (8/8 positive, p=0.0078) that
  failed the preregistered magnitude criterion** (INCONCLUSIVE/TIE).
- The asynchronous experiment is called **asynchrony/LOCF (Epps-like
  degradation)**, not a causal Epps demonstration.
- No trading, price-prediction or market-causality applicability is claimed.
- "No real market data were used" is stated in the abstract, title footnote,
  Section 11, Appendix A and the conclusion.
- The negative result is explicitly **not** a refutation of the seed paper's
  mathematics (Eser, Medeiros, Riza & Engel, arXiv:2507.08007).
